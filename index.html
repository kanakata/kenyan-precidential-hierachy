jomo kenyatta

1st el-patron

1964-1978

"The
Founding
Father"

life sory

Born Kamau wa Ngengi in the late 1890s, he was educated by missionaries. He spent 15 years in Europe (mostly London) lobbying for Kenyan land rights. Upon returning, he became a central figure in the independence movement. He was famously imprisoned as one of the "Kapenguria Six" during the Mau Mau Uprising, eventually becoming Kenya's first Prime Minister and then President from 1964.

key accomplishments

  • Unified the Nation: Promoted the philosophy of "Harambee" (all pull together) to unite various ethnic groups after colonial rule.
  • Education & Health: Launched the first major expansions of public schools and hospitals for indigenous Kenyans.
  • Economic Stability: Oversaw a period of significant economic growth and established Kenya as a pro-Western, stable regional hub.

fun fact : "mzee jomo kenyatta was a skilled carpenter before entering politics."

daniel moi

2nd el-patron

1978-2002

"The
Professor
Of Politics"

life story

A former schoolteacher from Baringo, Moi rose through the ranks to become Kenyatta’s Vice President. When Kenyatta died in 1978, Moi took over. He was known for his "Nyayo" (footsteps) philosophy—promising to follow Kenyatta’s path. He ruled for 24 years, making him Kenya’s longest-serving president.

key accomplishments

  • 8-4-4 System: Introduced the 8-4-4 education system to focus on more practical and technical skills.
  • Nyayo Milk: Launched a famous free milk program for primary school children to improve nutrition and school attendance.
  • Multi-party Democracy: Despite years of one-party rule, he eventually oversaw the transition back to multi-party politics in 1992 and a peaceful handover of power in 2002.

fun fact : "daniel torroitich arap moi never lost an election in his entire 47-year political career."

mwai kibaki

3rd el-patron

2002-2013

"The
Economic
Architect"

life story

A brilliant economist educated at Makerere University and the London School of Economics, Kibaki served as Finance Minister and Vice President before winning the presidency in a landslide in 2002. He was known for a "hands-off" leadership style, focusing on technocratic growth rather than political drama.

key accomplishments

  • Free Primary Education: One of his most celebrated feats, allowing millions of children to attend school for free.
  • The 2010 Constitution: He oversaw the promulgation of a new, modern Constitution that decentralized power to 47 counties (Devolution).
  • Infrastructure: Started the "Vision 2030" project, which led to the construction of the Thika Superhighway and other major transport links.

fun fact : "stanley emillio mwai kibaki, was the first African to graduate with a first-class degree from the London School of Economics."

uhuru kenyatta

4th el-patron

2013-2022

"The
Digital
President"

life story

The son of the first president, Uhuru was a businessman before entering politics. His presidency was marked by a focus on large-scale infrastructure and digital transformation. He was the first president to serve under the new 2010 Constitution for his entire term.

key accomplishments

  • SGR Railway: Built the Standard Gauge Railway (SGR) connecting Mombasa to Nairobi, the largest infrastructure project in Kenya’s history.
  • Big Four Agenda: Focused his second term on Universal Health Coverage, Affordable Housing, Manufacturing, and Food Security.
  • Electricity Connection: Massively expanded the "Last Mile" project, connecting millions of rural homes to the national power grid.

fun fact : "uhuru mwigai kenyatta is the first president in Kenya to be born after independence."

william ruto

5th el-patron

2022-Present

"The
Hustler"

life story

Born in rural Uasin Gishu, Ruto often spoke of his humble beginnings as a chicken seller. He rose through the kenyan political hirachy as a Member of Parliament and served as Deputy President for ten years before winning the 2022 election. He campaigned on a "Bottom-Up" economic model.

key accomplishments

  • The Hustler Fund: Launched a multi-billion shilling digital loan program to provide cheap credit to small-scale traders and individuals.
  • Subsidized Fertilizer: Focused on lowering the cost of farming inputs to boost food production and lower the cost of living.
  • Housing Projects: Initiated a massive affordable housing program aimed at creating jobs and providing low-cost homes in urban areas.

fun fact : "william samoei arap ruto used to walk barefoot to primary school in Kamagut."
